Why it matters for the hospital

  • Zero paperwork: replaces the manual Excel / whiteboard roster that consumes hours each week.
  • No coverage gaps: department-wise calendar makes missing shifts obvious at a glance.
  • Self-service swaps: doctors exchange duties in seconds, with the counterpart notified automatically — no admin chase-ups.
  • Accountability: every schedule change is logged for audit and dispute resolution.
