The Problem

  • Overwhelming administrative burden. Paper / Excel-based work consumes 3+ hours daily per professional; ~50% of doctor time goes to paperwork instead of patient care.
  • Communication gaps. Information silos across departments cause delayed referrals, missed follow-ups, duplicated effort and patient-safety risk.
  • Limited clinical context at the point of care. Doctors manually piece together scattered records, leading to reactive rather than proactive care.

The Solution

MedSync360 unifies the clinical workflow into one encrypted, doctor-validated platform: digital duty rostering, end-to-end referral & medication journeys, a one-click discharge summary, private 1:1 chat, and analytics — with AI features layered on a lightweight, progressive web app.

Live today WORKING

  • Duty management — weekly/calendar roster, shift types, department-wise organisation, self-service swaps with notifications.
  • Streamlined referrals — create with full patient detail, send/receive, status management (Received, Acknowledged, Closed, Cancelled), file attachments.
  • Medication journey & transfer chain — complete, auditable history across every hop.
  • One-click discharge summary — full diagnosis & medication history exported.
  • 1:1 encrypted chat — real-time messaging, presence, file sharing.
  • Analytics — disease-pattern & referral-closure insights.

Planned (AI roadmap)

  • AI Medical Transcription — ICD-10 coding with structured clinical notes; ~50% time saved, with confidence scoring.
  • AI Medical Assistant — clinical decision support, case assessment & planning.
  • Research Insights — curated healthcare news, drug innovation, case histories.
  • WhatsApp integration — patient updates across the admission-to-discharge cycle.
